Endorsement

Over the years, ABP has received different international endorsements for its quality education, operational excellence and commitment to the community. It also complies with all applicable regulations and standards in the industry

ABP is a registered Skills Training Provider under National Skills Development Authority Bangladesh (NSDA)

Edupro is a professional awarding body with a broad perspective to serve education worldwide.

ABP is the Learning and Exam Centre (LEC) of Edupro, UK.

Accounting and Auditing Organization for Islamic Financial Institutions (AAOIFI) is established in 1991 and based in Bahrain.

ABP is an approved Registration Centre of AAOIFI, Bahrain.

The CPD Certification Service is the largest and leading independent CPD accreditation organisation working across all industry sectors. CPD quality marks are protected by international copyright legislation. The certification process takes an impartial and objective overview of structure and value to ensure full conformity to CPD guidelines.

ABP is a Member of The CPD Certification Service. All the Postgraduate Diplomas of ABP are endorsed by CPD, UK.

Centre for Shari’ah Research of Islamic Banking and Finance –think tank for the development of products and services in Islamic Banking & Finance. ABP Offers several certificate courses in the field of Islamic Finance and Economics. These courses are endorsed by CSRIBF.

ISRA’s main research activities are focused on Islamic Banking, Islamic Capital Market, Takaful, and Fatwa and Translation.

ISRA was the knowledge partner of ABP for Professional Certificate in Structuring Sukuk course development.

The main objective of ICIFE is to increase and maintain the professionalism of the educators in Islamic Finance field at the national and international levels.

ICIFE endorsed the course Postgraduate Diploma in Islamic Finance Practices (PGDIFP), offered by ABP.

Grow Learn Connect is a platform for performance and learning professionals, trainers, advisory service providers, instructors, and others operating in emerging markets which has been governed by International Finance Corporation, World Bank Group. ABP is a member of the International Finance Corporation Learning Community and signatory to the GLC principles for learning.
