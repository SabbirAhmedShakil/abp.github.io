Course Content

Purpose of this Post Graduate Diploma in Islamic Banking & Finance course is not to explain how Islamic banks operate in Bangladesh. Rather, the purpose of this course is to explain he best practices of Islamic finance and banking as per Islami Shari'ah. Here is the content:

UNIT 1: SHARIAH ASPECTS OF BUSINESS & FINANCE
Session 01: Introduction to Fiqh-al-Muamalat
Session 02: Shariah Principles on Business Contracts
UNIT 2: ISLAMIC ECONOMICS
Session 01: Fundamentals of Islamic Economics
Session 02: Features of Islamic Economic System
UNIT 3: ISLAMIC SOCIAL FINANCE AND MICROFINANCE
Session 01: Fundamentals of Islamic Social Finance
Session 02: Application of Islamic Microfinance
UNIT 4: ISLAMIC FINANCE CONTRACTS
Session 01: Asset-backed financing Contracts
Session 02: Partnership based financing Contracts
UNIT 5: ISLAMIC BANKING OPERATIONS- INVESTMENT
Session 01: General Investment Products
Session 02: SME and Personal Financing Products
Session 03: Trade Finance Products
UNIT 6: ISLAMIC BANKING OPERATIONS- FINANCING
Session 01: General Deposit Products
Session 02: Treasury and Interbank Financing
UNIT 7: RISK MANAGEMENT
Session 01: Risk Management Concepts and Techniques in Islamic Finance
Session 02: Islamic Derivatives for Risk Management
UNIT 8: TAKAFUL AND RETAKAFUL
Session 01: Fundamentals of Takaful & Retakaful
Session 02: Models of Takaful & Retakaful
UNIT 9: FINANCIAL REPORTING AND SHARIAH AUDIT
Session 01: Fundamentals of Accounting for IFIs
Session 02: Financial Reporting for IFIs
Session 03: Accounting and Auditing issues of IFIs
UNIT 10: BLOCKCHAIN, FINTECH AND ISLAMIC BANKING
Session 01: Understanding Blockchain in Islamic Finance
Session 02: Application of Islamic Fintech
UNIT 11: GOVERNANCE AND COMPLIANCE OF ISLAMIC BANKING
Session 01: Governance and Compliance from Shariah perspective
Session 02: Regulatory Framework for Governance and Compliance
UNIT 12: ISLAMIC CAPITAL MARKET AND SUKUK
Session 01: Principles of Islamic Capital Market & Equity
Session 02: Sukuk & Islamic Funds
Session 03: Structuring Sukuk
